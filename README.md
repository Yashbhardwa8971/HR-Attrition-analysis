# 👨‍💼 HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations. This project analyzes HR employee data to identify the major factors influencing employee attrition using Python and data visualization techniques.

The analysis provides insights into employee demographics, income, job satisfaction, overtime, work-life balance, and departmental trends that affect employee retention.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Sales department has the highest employee attrition.
- Employees with lower monthly income have higher attrition.
- Employees with lower job satisfaction show higher resignation rates.
- Better work-life balance is associated with lower attrition.
- Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to resign.
- Income varies significantly across departments.
- Younger employees contribute more to employee turnover.
